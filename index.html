Anis' Atelier—

I believe numbers tell only half the story. For forty-plus years I balanced ledgers, navigated crises and grew banks across three continents.

The other half of the story hangs on these walls—brushstrokes that remind me value is more than basis points.

This site is a two-way corridor: walk one direction for a timeline of banking milestones; walk the other to step inside my art alcove. Somewhere in the middle, you will find the law firm I co-founded to keep value, risk and purpose in perfect tension.
